About us


The Alumni Association of ABSSR SCHOOL OF TECH was established in November 2006, under the visionary leadership of its founders, Mrs. ABC, the then President, and Mrs. RFT, the then Vice President. Originally named after the erstwhile CALTECH, the association was later rebranded to align with the school's new identity, ABSSR SCHOOL OF TECH. The association was founded with the noble aim of fostering a strong sense of community among former students, providing a platform for them to reconnect, share cherished memories, and collaborate on future aspirations. Today, the association thrives under the capable presidency of Mrs. NM and her dedicated team members, continuing to uphold the legacy of unity and camaraderie among alumni.
