Welcome to REUNION 2024!


The ABSSR SCHOOL OF TECH is thrilled to present REUNION, our long-awaited reunion! It's a joy to see familiar faces and reconnect after so much time. Today, we celebrate cherished memories, rekindle old friendships, and create new ones. Let's share stories, laughter, and perhaps a few tears, as we reminisce about the past and look forward to the future. Here's to the bonds that have stood the test of time and the wonderful moments ahead. Enjoy every minute of this special gathering!
